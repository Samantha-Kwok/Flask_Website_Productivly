"It always seems impossible until it is done." once said by Nelson Mandela. There is not a better statement than this to reflect the creation of this website-"Productivly".


Prodctivly, as the first website I built from scratch, enables myself and other users to build a To-Do-List digitally, as well as share our general thoughts on personal development.

Why Productivly then? Programing and website development has been at the top of the list of "Things-To-Try" for about 7 years.

In Decemeber 2019, I made a concious decision to take a career break. Taking a break is not natural to me. I have thought I got to achieve or improve somthing everyday. Back then,I hardly asked myself questions like "Am I happy?" or "Am I stressed?". That is why I stepped out and took time to explore what makes me feel happy and productive. Then I dived into the list of "Things-To-Try". After taking 2-month of online programing courses, I applied what I learnt into practices and built Productivly. it is a journey full of trials and errors (it kept me motivated during the global pandemic.)
